Amal Clooney Steps Out in Red Jumpsuit to Attend Day 2 of Meghan Markles NYC Baby Shower See

Since Amal attended her famous friend’s lavish royal wedding in May 2018, she, of course, cleared her busy schedule to celebrate Meg with a baby shower in the Big Apple, too!

It was a very natural friendship from day one.”

Meghan’s husband, Prince Harry, is also very good friends with George, 57. In April 2018, Harry and Meghan jetted off to Italy to vacation with the Clooneys at their house in Lake Como. “George and Amal were so happy to host Meghan and Harry.
