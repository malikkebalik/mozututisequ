What is Bill Dundee's Net Worth?

Bill Dundee is an Australian professional wrestler and author who has a net worth of $1 million. Bill Dundee was born in Dundee, Scotland in October 1943. He was raised in Melbourne, Australia and became a trapeze artist in the circus at 16 years old. Dundee started to wrestle in 1962 and then moved to the United States as "Superstar" Bill Dundee in 1974. He wrestled in the Memphis Territory and teamed and feuded with Jimmy Valiant and Jerry Lawler. Dundee went to the American Wrestling Association where the won the AWA World Tag Team Titles twice with Lawler.

Dundee was the Southern Heavyweight Champion many times and also wrestled in NWA's Jim Crocket Promotions, Florida Championship Wrestling, and Central States Wrestling. He was known as Sir William in the early 1990s in World Championship Wrestling. Dundee now works as a booker and promotes wrestling and MMA fighting as well as being a Marketing Director for Cole Brothers Circus. His son Jamie Dundee is a professional wrestler as well as his son-in-law Bobby Eaton. He authored the book If You Don't Want The Answer, Don't Ask The Question: Bill Dundee's Life Story.
