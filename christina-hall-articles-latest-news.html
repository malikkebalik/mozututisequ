Christina Hall | Articles &amp; Latest News

Christina Hall is a reality star and real estate investor who rose to fame on HGTV’s Flip or Flop. The show premiered in 2013 and followed Christina and now ex-husband Tarek El Moussa as they flipped houses in the Orange County, California, area. Tarek focused on the financials and the structural updates to the homes, while Christina focused primarily on design. Following her divorce from Tarek in 2018, HGTV announced that Christina would receive her own spinoff called Christina on the Coast. The show premiered in 2019 and originally followed Christina as she remodeled her new home following her divorce. The show then pivoted to Christina's work updating the homes of other clients in the Southern California area. Christina also released a limited series via Discovery+ called Christina: Stronger By Design that focused more on her personal life. Following divorces from Tarek El Moussa and later Ant Anstead, Christina married Josh Hall in 2022. The two now have an additional spinoff, Christina in the Country, filmed in Nashville.

Christina Hall biographical info

  • Born: July 9, 1983
  • Spouse: Josh Hall (m. 2022); Ant Anstead (m. 2019-2021); Tarek El Moussa (m. 2009-2018)
  • Children: Hudson London Anstead (b. 2019), Brayden El Moussa (b. 2015), Taylor El Moussa (b. 2010)
  • Net Worth: $25 million
