K- pop Singer Nahee Instagram: Which Post of Her Went Viral?

We are deeply saddened to share with you that K-pop singer “Nahee” has passed away at the age of 24. After the news of her death broke out, her Instagram post went viral and in this post, you will get all the information related to it.

Which Instagram Post Of K- pop Singer Nahee Went Viral?

A Korean singer-songwriter named Nahee died when she was 24 years old. The funeral for Nahee will take place at the Central Funeral Hall in Pyeongtaek, Gyeonggi-do.

As soon as news of her demise broke, her last post on Instagram went viral and is now widely shared. This post was about the day before she died.

There were a lot of pictures in the post including a cute selfie of the singer. After that, there was a glimpse of a train ride that she must have taken not long ago. She also put up a few pictures of her dog but didn’t write anything about them. You can see her last post provided below:

Fans have written a lot of comments on Nahee’s last post about how they feel about her death. Reacting to the news, one fan wrote in the comment section, “This is crazy. rest in peace beautiful.” 

Another fan wrote, “The little dog is now alone… oh my God rest in peace.” Someone else said, “Sad to have discovered such an angelic talented voice, your music will live on and attract new fans like me.” Some fans shared their condolences on social media.

Has Nahee’s Cause of Death Revealed?

Nahee’s cause of death is currently undisclosed. Neither her agency nor her family has issued an official statement regarding the cause of Nahee’s death.

On Friday, Nov. 10, the idol-pop singer’s death was announced by her record label Mun Hwa on social media. The statement from the label’s official Instagram account read:

“Singer-songwriter Nahee has left our earth and become a star in the sky. I am sorry to bring sudden news to all of you fans who have supported and loved Nahee.”
