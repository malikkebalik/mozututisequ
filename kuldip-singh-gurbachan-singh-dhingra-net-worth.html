What is Kuldip Singh & Gurbachan Singh Dhingra's Net Worth?

Kuldip Singh & Gurbachan Singh Dhingra are Indian brothers and businessmen who have a net worth of $2.2 billion. Kuldip Singh & Gurbachan Singh Dhingra have a controlling stake of 75% of Berger Paints India which is the 2nd largest paint maker in India. The company was formed in 1923 and the brothers bought it from liquor tycoon Vijay Mallya in 1991. Berger Paints also operates in Russia, Poland, Nepal, Cyprus, and Bangladesh.

Kuldip Singh Dhingra was born in 1947 and is the Chairman of Berger Paints. Gurbachan Singh Dhingra was born in 1950 and is the Vice Chairman of Berger Paints. Both brothers graduated from Delhi University. They own a farm near Delhi.
