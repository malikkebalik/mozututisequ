What is Mike Dean's Net Worth?

Mike Dean is an American hip hop record producer, audio engineer, and musician who has a net worth of $8 million. Mike Dean was born in Houston, Texas in March 1965. He has specialized in the genres of hip hop, R&B, pop, and trap and plays guitar, keyboards, cello, bass, and bassoon.

Dean has produced albums for artists including 5th Ward Boyz, Beyonce, Big Mike, Daz Dillinger, Tha Dogg Pound, E-40, Ganksta N-I-P, Geto Boys, G.O.O.D. Music, Jay-Z, Juvenile, Kanye West, Kid Cudi, Madonna, Mike Jones, Pimp C, Scarface, The Weeknd, Travis Scott, Ty Dolla Sign, Yung Lean, and Z-Ro. Dean has won several Grammy Awards including Best Rap Album for Late Registration in 2006, Best Rap Album for Graduation in 2008, Best Rap Song for "Good Life" in 2008, Best Rap Album for My Beautiful Dark Twisted Fantasy in 2012, and Best Rap Song for "Ni**as In Paris" in 2013.
