Mike Posner | Lifestyle

Mike Posner is best known for the song I Took A Pill In Ibiza, which peaked at number one for four weeks in 2016.

After his father died of cancer on January 11, 2017, the actor put up his old habits, abstaining from alcohol and narcotics, and going on a life-altering adventure.

He began walking on April 15 in Asbury Park, New Jersey, and didn’t stop for six months until he reached the Pacific Ocean.

Posner walked 2,851 miles (4,588 kilometers) during his journey, rising at 4 a.m. every day and walking up to 30 miles each day.

He was bitten by a rattlesnake along the route and had to be airlifted to a Colorado hospital, where he was warned he would lose his leg.

However, he somehow continued his expedition three weeks later, from exactly where he left off.

Mike Posner | Charity

Mike Posner climbed Mount Everest in commemoration of his late father as part of a charity effort.

The singer-songwriter announced the news on Twitter on 1st June 2021, stating that he and his guides, Dr. Jon Kedrowski, Dawa Chirring Sherpa, and Dawa Dorje Sherpa, reached the summit at 4:35 a.m. local time.

Posner stated in April that he would attempt to climb Mount Everest to raise money for the Detroit Justice Center.

He credited his choice to seek funding for the center to his late father’s work as an experienced defense attorney in the city.

Posner was inspired to climb Everest midway through his ambition to walk across America in 2019, which he completed in just six months.

Dr. Jon Kedrowski, who has completed multiple Everest trips, spent a year and a half preparing for the summit alongside the singer-songwriter.

At the time of writing, a GoFundMe set up for Posner’s fundraiser had generated more than $223,724 (£158,407), with an ultimate goal of $250,000 (£176,976).

Mike Posner | Career

Posner began his career in music as a record producer. In 2009, he rose to prominence after releasing the mixtapes A Matter of Time’ and ‘One Foot Out the Door.’

He released his debut album, ’31 Minutes to Takeoff,’ the following year, featuring the singles ‘Cooler Than Me,’ ‘Bow Chicka Wow,’ and ‘Please Don’t Go.’

Posner began work on his second album, initially titled ‘Sky High,’ after the album’s release, and released the single ‘Looks Like Sex’ in December 2011.

His third mixtape, named ‘The Layover,’ was released after that.

In March 2017, he founded a hip hop and R&B duet Mansionz with American singer Blackbear.

Cyhi The Prynce, Soren Bryce, G-Eazy, Snooze God, Dennis Rodman, and Spark Master Tape all had cameos on their self-titled debut album, released on March 24.

Posner co-wrote Justin Bieber’s song “Boyfriend,” Maroon 5’s “Sugar,” and Labrinth’s “Beneath Your Beautiful,” among many other famous songs.
