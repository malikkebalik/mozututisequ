Solveig Karadottir Career

Solveig Karadottir is a fashion designer in London. She is the Creative Director and co-founder of Galvan London, a label established in 2014.

Solveig, along with Carolyn Hodler, Katherine Holmgren, and Anna-Christina Haas, started Galvan London with the aim of creating modern, clean, and stunning eveningwear, including dresses and jumpsuits.

They also design simple and casual wedding attire. Aside from her fashion career, Solveig is known to have worked as a model in her teenage years. Although she left modeling to explore other opportunities, she has been featured in magazines like Eskimo.

Currently, she is fully dedicated to her role as a fashion designer. After her divorce from Dhani Harrison in 2016, Solveig, the former model, is now leading a single life.
