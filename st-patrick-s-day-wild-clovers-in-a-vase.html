St. Patricks Day Wild Clovers in a Vase

Wild Shamrocks in a Vase

The below idea incorporates the St. Patrick’s Day symbol of the shamrock. Visit our St. Patrick’s Day Symbols page to learn more about the symbolism and meaning of the shamrock for St. Patrick’s Day.

Supplies:

Wild Shamrocks (Clovers)

Small Vase

Directions:

Why buy flowers when wild shamrocks (clovers) are growing all over at this time of year? If you live in a place that is not snowed over in mid-March, take a little walk outside to find some wild clovers. Display them in a pretty vase for a lovely centerpiece.
