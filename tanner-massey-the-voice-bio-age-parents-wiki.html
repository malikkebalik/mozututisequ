Tanner Massey (The Voice) Bio, Age, Parents, Wiki

Tanner Massey, a contestant on “The Voice,” left a lasting impression with his singing prowess. His performance led three coaches to turn their chairs during the audition, ultimately joining Team Niall. With a versatile and uniquely captivating voice, Tanner has garnered a massive fan base, showcasing his exceptional talent on the show.

Tanner Massey was born on 3rd June 2004 in Choctaw, Oklahoma. Clayton Massey and Jean Massey are his parents and he grew up with his two sisters Anna and Ainsley. At the age of 11, Tanner’s singing talent caught his parents’ attention, prompting him to start working with a vocal coach soon after. In 2021, he signed a one-year developmental deal with UMG, through the music label 1251 Ave.

Tanner is also a member of Acapop! KIDS, is an acapella group co-founded by Pentatonix’s Scott Hoying. One of his notable projects includes a cover of “Shallow,” with a music video surpassing 15 million views. This rendition stands as Acapop! KIDS’ most-watched and highly liked video.

Tanner’s vocal talents have graced local opry houses like the Rodeo Opry and Heartland Opry across Oklahoma, along with numerous regional festivals. His acclaim soared at the McSwain Theatre in Ada, where he clinched ‘Best under 16’ titles for two consecutive years at their Grand Awards Show in 2017 and 2019. Notably, he performed the National Anthem for the Oklahoma City Thunder Basketball Team, the Oklahoma City Dodgers minor league baseball team, and the Big 12 Championship College Baseball game in 2017.

In June 2022, he released his debut independent single, “Break Me,” available on major platforms. With multiple singles under his belt in the past 6 years, Tanner is diligently crafting his first album while splitting time between Los Angeles and other locations. “Break Me” explores themes of resilience in the face of challenges and anxiety, embodying Tanner’s pop-infused style.

The Voice

Tanner’s career soared when he joined “The Voice.” His rendition of “Before You Go” captivated judges Niall Horan, Reba McEntire, John Legend, and Gwen Stefani, prompting three of them to turn their chairs in admiration. His journey on the show is truly inspiring, earning him a massive fan base due to his remarkable performance. The audience was entranced by Tanner’s emotive and profound rendition of the song, radiating raw emotion and depth.
