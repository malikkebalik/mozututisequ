What's Pete Davidson's Net Worth in 2020

Pete Davidson is one of the most-talked-about actors and comedians in Hollywood. Outside of his work, his personal life has also been subject to many headlines, especially over the past few years. Due to his many projects, he has accrued several millions of dollars.

What has Pete Davidson been up to lately?

Davidson dominated headlines in 2018 for his whirlwind relationship with Ariana Grande. He became more than those headlines in 2019 when he continued to make news as a cast member on Saturday Night Live.

He has also been open about having borderline personality disorder. In a December Instagram post, he said, “I’ve kept my mouth shut. Never mentioned any names, never said a word about anyone or anything. I’m trying to understand how when something happens to a guy, the whole entire world just trashes him without any facts or frame of reference.

He continued, “Especially in today’s climate, where everyone loves to be offended and upset, it truly is mind-boggling. I’ve been getting online bullied and in public by people for nine months. I’ve spoken about BPD and being suicidal publicly only in the hopes that it will help bring awareness and help kids like myself who don’t want to be on this earth.I just want you guys to know. No matter how hard the internet or anyone tries to make me kill myself, I won’t. I’m upset I even have to say this. To all those holding me down and seeing this for what it is — I see you and I love you.”

Davidson’s upcoming roles include a role in James Gunn’s The Suicide Squad. The DC Comics film also stars Margot Robbie, Viola Davis, Joel Kinnaman, Idris Elba, and Storm Reid. Due to is work as an actor and comedian, Davidson has a net worth of $4 million, according to Celebrity Net Worth.

Pete Davidson and Kaia Gerber just broke up

Recently, Davidson and his most recenter girlfriend, Kaia Gerber, split.

After Grande, Davidson was also romantically linked to actresses Margaret Qualley and Kate Beckinsale.

“It looks like Pete and Kaia are over. It got very overwhelming for Kaia. Pete has a certain M.O. and he’s very intense to his girlfriends. Kaia is only 18 and it’s a lot to deal with,” a source told Page Six.

He also has a movie coming out

Davidson has a biopic that will be released soon. The film, King of Staten Island, will be released this year and it will premiere at the South by Southwest (SXSW) Film Festival.

The movie is directed by Judd Apatow and stars Davidson, Maude Apatow, Bel Powley, Marisa Tomei, Jimmy Tatro, Colson Baker (aka Machine Gun Kelly), Moisés Arias and many others.

The official SXSW description of the film describes it as “a bracing comedy about a burnout stuck living with his mom.”

“Judd Apatow has consistently provided transcendent, exhilarating experiences at SXSW, starting with Knocked Up, then Bridesmaids, Trainwreck, Girls, The Big Sick, and his documentary May it Last: A Portrait of The Avett Brothers. “ his March, it’s our particular privilege to present his funny and deeply moving new feature — his first in five years as a director — The King of Staten Island, starring Pete Davidson leading a great ensemble, as our Opening Night Film,” said Janet Pierson, the festival’s Director of Film, in a statement.

After premiering at the festival in March, it will have a theatrical release via Universal Pictures in June.
