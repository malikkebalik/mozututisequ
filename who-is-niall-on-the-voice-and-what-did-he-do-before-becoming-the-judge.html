How did Niall become The Voice’s Judge?

Niall is a member of The Voice’s judging panel. Judges Blake Shelton, Kelly Clarkson, and Chance the Rapper join Niall.

In contrast to many other singing reality competition series, the judges on The Voice not only provide feedback on each contestant’s performance but also form a team of their choosing and coach them throughout the season.

The number of members on the team ranges from three to five, depending on how many people made it past the first round of testing. At the beginning of the presentation, the judges will have their backs on the candidate while listening to them; if they like what they hear, they can press a button on their chairs to turn around and offer the person an invitation to join their team.

Niall’s unorthodox ploy to win over candidate Ross Clayton is a big reason why he’s been in the news so much recently. The performer just walked up to each chair and touched the button again, turning the judges around.
