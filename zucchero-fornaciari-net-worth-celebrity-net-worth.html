What is Adelmo Fornaciari's Net Worth?

Adelmo Fornaciari is an Italian singer-songwriter and musician who has a net worth of $85 million. Adelmo Fornaciari was born in Roncocesi, Reggio Emilia, Italy in September 1955. He is known by his stage name Zucchero. His music blends several styles including blues, R&B, rock, soul, funk, pop, and gospel. Fornaciari has been called the "father of the Italian blues".

He has sold more than 60 million records in three decades. Fornaciari has won nine Wind Music Awards, four Festivalbar awards, and two World Music Awards. He has also been nominated for a Grammy Award. Zucchero has collaborated with several artists including Eric Clapton, Stevie Ray Vaughan, Jeff Beck, Miles Davis, B.B. King, Brian May, Ray Charles, Sting, Paul Young, Bono, Peter Gabriel, Andrea Bocelli, and Luciano Pavarotti.  He has released 12 studio albums including the albums Blue's, Oro incense e birra, Miserere, Spirito DiVino, BlueSugar, Shake, Fly, Chocabeck, and Black Cat which all reached #1 in Italy. His most successful singles include "Senza una donna (Without a Woman)" (feat. Paul Young) and "Baila (Sexy Thing)".
